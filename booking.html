The Kategoria Superiore, officially known as Abissnet Superiore for sponsorship reasons, is a professional league for men's association football clubs. At the top of the Albanian football league system, it is the country's primary football competition.
Most championships: Tirana (25 titles)
Domestic cup(s): Albanian Cup; Albanian Supercup
